## **Daily Yogic Sadhana Plan**

### **Morning Ritual (Isha-inspired, 45 min)**

* **Asanas** (5–7 min):

  * Butterfly pose
  * Baby cradle
  * Cat–Cow
* **Pranayama & Bandhas** (15 min):

  * Anulom Vilom – 2 min
  * Omkar chanting – 21 times
  * Fast shallow breathing – 120 breaths
  * Inhale & exhale with 3 bandhas
* **Dhyana** (Stillness) – 6–7 min

### **Day Practice**

* **Physical & Mudra Activation**:

  * Warm water on waking
  * PraaN Ling Mudra – 5 min
  * Energetic clapping – 2 min
* **Discipline**:

  * Digital & food fasting – 2 days/week
* **Mental Clarity**:

  * Study modern AI/code updates – focused session

### **Evening Flow (Shrimath-inspired, 60–70 min)**

* **Pranayama & Inner Work**:

  * Bhramari – 6–7 min
  * Antar Maun (Inner Silence) – 20 min
  * Yoga Nidra – 30 min
* **Sattvic Enrichment**:

  * Flute (long notes) – 10 min
  * Strategy/insight books – 20 min

### **Night Wind-Down**

* Ghee in nostrils & on soles
* Mantra Japa: "Om Shree Maatre Namah" – softly till sleep
* Deep sleep – 7 hours
* If waking early:

  * Chant Ram Naam with belly focus – 63 times

### **Idle/Waiting Moments**

* Practice **Anapan-sati**: Witness breath silently

---

## **Core Sankalpa (Resolution)**

* Sound sleep. Strong body. Graceful mind.
* **Clarity (बुद्धी), Strength (शक्ति), Presence (स्वरूप)**
* From **Shakti → Shanti → Ishwar**
* Detachment = Witness = Ishwar
* Life = Final birth: Live as a Yogi.
* Accept distractions, stand inside difficulties, remain aware.
